Post Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Caste Students (PMS-SC): Objective: Provide financial assistance to SC students pursuing post-secondary education. Benefits: Covers maintenance allowance, reimbursement of compulsory non-refundable fees, and other allowances. Eligibility: SC students studying at post-matriculation or post-secondary levels. Implementing Body: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.
National Fellowship for Scheduled Caste Students: Objective: Encourage SC students to pursue higher education leading to M.Phil. and Ph.D. degrees. Benefits: Provides fellowship for research leading to M.Phil. and Ph.D. degrees. Eligibility: SC candidates who have qualified for Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) through UGC-NET or UGC-CSIR-NET. Implementing Body: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.
Stand Up India Scheme: Objective: Facilitate bank loans to SC/ST and women entrepreneurs for setting up greenfield enterprises. Benefits: Provides bank loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 crore for setting up new enterprises in manufacturing, services, or trading sectors. Eligibility: SC/ST and women entrepreneurs above 18 years of age. Implementing Body: Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance.
National Overseas Scholarship for Scheduled Tribe Students: Objective: Provide financial assistance to ST students for pursuing higher studies abroad. Benefits: Covers tuition fees, living expenses, and other related costs for postgraduate and doctoral studies abroad. Eligibility: ST candidates with a minimum of 55% marks in the qualifying examination and below 35 years of age. Implementing Body: Ministry of Tribal Affairs.
Adivasi Mahila Sashaktikaran Yojana: Objective: Empower tribal women by providing concessional finance for income-generating activities. Benefits: Offers loans up to ₹1 lakh at a concessional interest rate of 4% per annum. Eligibility: Scheduled Tribe women above 18 years of age. Implementing Body: National Scheduled Tribes Finance and Development Corporation (NSTFDC).
Venture Capital Fund for Scheduled Castes: Objective: Promote entrepreneurship among SCs by providing concessional finance to SC entrepreneurs. Benefits: Equity finance up to ₹15 crore for a maximum of 15 years. Eligibility: SC entrepreneurs with at least 51% shareholding by SC promoters in the company. Implementing Body: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.
Pre-Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Tribe Students: Objective: Support ST students studying in classes IX and X to reduce dropout rates. Benefits: Scholarship amount covering tuition fees, maintenance allowance, and other allowances. Eligibility: ST students studying in classes IX and X with parental income not exceeding ₹2 lakh per annum. Implementing Body: Ministry of Tribal Affairs.
Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS): Objective: Support the economic development of STs by providing funds for income-generating schemes. Benefits: Financial assistance for various projects aimed at enhancing the livelihood of ST communities. Eligibility: Projects benefiting Scheduled Tribe individuals and communities. Implementing Body: Ministry of Tribal Affairs.
